Getting Started with your Device


  1. Sign in to your Amazon account with your user name and password. If you don't have an account go to Amazon.com, click on Sign In, then follow the steps as a new customer to create an account.
  2. Now the fire stick will try to connect to the Internet. Go to Network Settings and connect to your wifi connection
  3. Once on the main menu scroll down to system, go left to application, then down to Mange Installed Applications, then down to Kodi.
  4. Once into Kodi, wait about 3 minutes for all apps to load and update. Now you are ready to use your device!

*If you notice your device freezes for any reason click the Home Button on your fire stick remote and go back to where the Kodi app is and select "Force Close". Once clicked it will close Kodi and you can select "Launch Application"

- Device will freeze if Kodi hasn't been run after awhile. Best advice is to run Kodi at least every other day so apps can update regularly instead of being forced to update all at once.
